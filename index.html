Garbage Finder

Garbage Finder is a website that aims to tackle the global littering problem by helping people locate and report instances of garbage in their vicinity. The website provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily search for garbage in their area by entering their location or using the website's GPS function.

Testimonial

As I surveyed my neighborhood, I was struck by the prevalence of garbage and litter that marred its beauty. It became apparent to me that many people litter simply because they lack the motivation to seek out a garbage can and dispose of their trash properly. This realization was the impetus for our creation of Garbage Finder. By pinpointing the nearest available garbage can, we aim to motivate individuals to make use of it and, in turn, reduce littering. Through the use of Garbage Finder, we can guarantee a cleaner, more attractive neighborhood for all.

Kahlil
